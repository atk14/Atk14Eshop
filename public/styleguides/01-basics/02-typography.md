## Headings

There are also classes <code>.h1</code>&hellip;<code>.h6</code> to achieve the same appearance.
Headings appearance may vary across various components and contexts.

[example]
<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<h3>Heading 3</h3>
<h4>Heading 4</h4>
<h5>Heading 5</h5>
<h6>Heading 6</h6>
[/example]
